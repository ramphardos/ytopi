ABOUT

Pokémon cards are something that you enjoy because you want to, and there's so much for you to want in them. It could be the joy of collecting, battling, or even the sense of community, but whatever it is, it's supposed to be fun, and above all, Pokémon cards are fun. I like Pokemon because it is really fun to trade and collect. Some of my best cards are Dragonite V, Scrafty, and Purugly. Pokemon is also a good show because there are many adventures and battles. The rarest card I own is venasaur v and yeveltal gx. This is why I like Pokemon.
